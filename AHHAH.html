We are excited to announce, Self-Service for students launched Monday, April 4, 2022. During this initial rollout, Self-Service will provide:

  • Student Planning
    • Search and register for classes
    • View class schedule
    • Add or drop classes directly from the schedule
    • Plan classes
    • Request a change to your program of study
  • Course Catalog
    • Browse classes
  • Student Finance
    • View and print account statements
    • Make payments

eConnect will still be available until the final pieces are migrated into Self-Service.
